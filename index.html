A rgeta Birhanu is model farmer in Bullen, Metekel , Ethiopia. Starting from 1990 E.C he started projects which focuses on making bare lands green . The name of the project was FINONE.In order to to achive his goal he started by planting mango(Mangifera indica) on one and half Hectar farm lands from 1998-2002 E.C . Starting from 2003-2007 he had planted wanza(Cordia Africana) on two Hectar farm lands. In 2005-2009 E.C he had been planting Bamboo(Bambusa vulgaris) approximatly onfour Hectar lands in Baruda kebele Bullen, Ethiopia.In 2007 E.C he also planted Bahir Zaf(eucalyptus tree ) on one (1) Hectar farm land. Even though he had got difficulties in cultivating such amaizing plant in that remote area , he didn't stop his work in order to achive what he intend .
